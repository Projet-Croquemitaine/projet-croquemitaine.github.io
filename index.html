Hello! I'm Côme Serlooten

I'm a Junior Game Programmer engineer, with a passion for creating fun and engaging experiences and games, welcome to my portfolio. Feel free to explore!

About me :

I'm an artist, designer, storyteller and game programer with experience in Unity and Unreal, on PC, Mobile and VR, always on the lookout for new ways to express my creativity and bring ideas to life.


Programer and Tech-oriented by my studies, I do my best to integrate my artistic skills into all my project to give them that extra spark of life. My projects are mostly based on Unity and Unreal Engine, often enriched with my own self-made sprites and 2D animations, and explore PC, mobile, and VR platforms.

With the chance to have lived in and visited many countries, and studied in France, England, and Canada during my master's degrees, I hope to draw from all the places I've seen and people I've met, as well as from all the worlds I've discovered to craft unique, compelling, and engaging experiences for players to explore with me.
